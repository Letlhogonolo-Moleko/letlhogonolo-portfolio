POS System Enhancement

Business Need

The business faced operational inefficiencies including frequent stockouts, slow transactions, and long queues during peak hours. There was also limited visibility into sales trends and inventory data, affecting strategic decisions. These challenges made it essential to optimize the POS system.

Role & Responsibilities

As the Business Analyst, I led the information gathering process which included interviewing stakeholders, analyzing existing processes, and identifying pain points. I documented requirements and helped design optimized solutions for the POS system.

AS-IS Analysis

I conducted a process mapping session using BPMN to understand the existing workflow. Bottlenecks included manual inventory updates, fragmented systems, and lack of real-time reporting. Key stakeholders expressed frustration with frequent data entry errors and delays in order processing.

Objectives

  • Reduce long customer lines and wait times
  • Increase order accuracy
  • Boost sales and revenue
  • Enable real-time inventory management
  • Improve payment processing efficiency
  • Enhance data collection for better decision-making

Proposed TO-BE Solution

The improved POS system integrates with inventory, billing, and reporting modules. Key features include real-time stock updates, barcode scanning, faster checkouts, and automated end-of-day reporting. A streamlined dashboard was proposed for better performance insights.

Techniques Used

  • Stakeholder interviews with cashiers, managers, and suppliers
  • Surveys to gather user pain points and wishlists
  • SWOT analysis to identify improvement areas
  • User stories and acceptance criteria documentation

Technologies & Tools

Business process modeling, stakeholder analysis, and documentation tools were used to design and propose system enhancements. The proposed technical implementation included POS terminals, inventory tracking systems, and integration with reporting dashboards.

Projected Impact

  • 30% reduction in customer wait time
  • 20% improvement in order accuracy
  • Real-time visibility reduced stockouts by 40%
  • Faster processing increased daily sales capacity by 15%

Lessons Learned

This project taught me the importance of involving end-users early in the design phase and validating assumptions with real data. I also deepened my understanding of how system integrations can directly impact business performance.
